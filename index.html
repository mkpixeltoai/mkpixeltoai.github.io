01 — About

About Me

My creative journey started in 2004 with Photoshop, where I specialized in photo editing, retouching, wedding albums, and photo restoration.

In 2009 I expanded into CorelDRAW, designing business cards, wedding invitations, brochures, flyers, and print materials. In 2011 I moved into Web Design using HTML, CSS, PHP, and WordPress.

Since 2015 I have been creating cinematic wedding films, event videos, music videos, commercials, and professional edits. Today I combine all of my creative experience with AI to create product commercials, AI models, AI story videos, AI music videos, AI animation, and promotional content.

Case Studies

A closer look at three projects: the brief, the approach, and the measurable result.

Photo Restoration,Black & White to Color,Damaged Photo Repair

Advanced Photo Restoration & Manual Colorization

Adobe Photoshop

2004–2009

Project Overview

From 2004 to 2009, I specialized in professional photo restoration and manual colorization using Adobe Photoshop. My work included restoring damaged family photographs, repairing torn images, reconstructing missing facial features, converting black-and-white photographs into realistic color portraits, and preparing high-quality print-ready images. Every project was completed manually with precision and attention to detail.

Challenge

Many photographs were heavily damaged due to age, scratches, fading, torn paper, missing corners, and even partially missing faces. Since AI tools were not available at that time, every correction had to be completed manually while preserving the originality of the image.

Creative Process

  • Image Inspection
  • Dust & Scratch Removal
  • Clone Stamp Restoration
  • Healing Brush Cleanup
  • Half Face Reconstruction
  • Manual Black & White Colorization
  • Color Correction
  • Final Print Preparation

Software Used

  • Adobe Photoshop
  • Clone Stamp Tool
  • Healing Brush
  • Layer Masks
  • Curves
  • Levels
  • Color Balance

Services Delivered

  • Old Photo Restoration
  • Black & White to Color
  • Wedding Portrait Retouching
  • Damaged Photo Repair
  • Half Face Reconstruction
  • Print Ready Images

Results

Successfully restored numerous family memories by repairing damaged photographs, manually colorizing black-and-white images, and delivering print-ready photographs suitable for albums and framing.

Portfolio Note

Most projects shown in this case study were completed between 2004 and 2009. Because these projects are over two decades old, many original client files are no longer available. The work presented represents the real techniques and professional workflow used during that period.

Career Evolution

The experience gained from manual photo restoration became the foundation of my future work in print design, web development, cinematic video editing, and AI-powered content creation. Today I combine traditional creative skills with modern AI technologies to produce premium visual experiences.
